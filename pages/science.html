Important Science Terms

Here are some important terms you'll encounter in your science journey:

Atom
The basic unit of matter, consisting of a nucleus (protons and neutrons) and electrons surrounding it.
Gravity
The force that attracts objects towards the center of the Earth, or any other physical body having mass.
Photosynthesis
The process by which plants use sunlight to synthesize foods from carbon dioxide and water.
Evaporation
The process where liquid water turns into vapor or gas due to heat.
Habitat
The natural environment where a species lives, including all living and non-living factors.
